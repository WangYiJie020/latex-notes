From Delta to Wye we have
\[
	R_{an} = \frac{R_b R_c}{R_a + R_b + R_c}
\] 
From Wye to Delta we have
\[
	R_a = R_{bn} + R_{cn} + \frac{R_{bn} R_{cn}}{R_{an}}
\] 

\section{Capacitors and Inductors}

The energy stored in a capacitor is given by
\[
	w_C = \frac{1}{2} C V^2 = \frac{Q^2}{2C}
\] 
\subsection{Inductors}
Solenoid has an inductance of
\[
	L=\mu \frac{N^2 A}{l}
\] 
where $N$ is the number of turns, $A$ is the cross-sectional area, and $l$ is
the length of the solenoid. 

\[
	w_L = \frac{1}{2} L I^2
\]
